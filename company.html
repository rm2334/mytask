EON takes the pain out of mortgage shopping.

Shopping for a new home or planning a home upgrade is fun and filled with excitement. Shopping for a mortgage or refi to finance those life changes is often a chore, one filled with paperwork and anxiety.

We get it. Your EON mortgage professionals are more than experts: we are your guides, available at every step of the process to answer questions and help.

How We Work

We start with your goals – a new primary residence, a new addition to your house, consolidating your debt, finding a good investment property – and a review of your current financial situation. We can quickly advise you on your financing options, matching your resources and goals to the best type of loan. We then contact multiple lenders to find the absolute best rates and terms available for you.

We don’t stop there. EON will guide you through the underwriting process all the way through closing. And we stay with you after the close to make sure you are happy with the program. EON has a handy tool for you: an online dashboard that shows you the current status of your loan. And EON will periodically send you information on trends and developments in mortgage lending so you can take advantage of any changes in interest rates or terms.

Our personal commitment

EON mortgage brokers believe in the goal of home ownership and profitable real estate investments. We commit ourselves to providing you with fast, efficient and friendly service and clear and transparent communications.

Jannie Gong

Jannie Gong is a founder of EON Mortgage Group, and is Principal Mortgage Broker for the company.

Jannie has been in love with the Chicago area since she and her family emigrated from China. She attended University of Illinois, Chicago. After graduation, she worked for mortgage brokerage firms in Chicagoland.

Jannie loves her work. “I’m a people person,” she said, “but I also have a strong analytical mind. That combination is perfect for the mortgage business. When I found someone with the same capabilities and drive –Steve- we started EON.”

“My favorite relationship is to work with a homebuyer or investor through the entire financing process, so I can fully understand their goals. Then I work hard to make sure they realize their dreams.” Jannie’s commitment to Chicago goes beyond her mortgage work: she frequently volunteers with local nonprofits to improve the community.

Steve Song

Steve Song is a founder of EON Mortgage Group, responsible for the finances, marketing, and direction of the company.

Steve is an entrepreneur, investor and business professional with a background in technology and information management. After corporate positions with a Top 10 accounting firm and a Fortune 500 company, Steve made a series of investments, including in real estate. The process of financing his real estate purchases changed his career path.

“Getting a mortgage was a frustrating experience,” he said. “While the brokers were pleasant people, I was always chasing them for information. And I sensed that they were more interested in promoting certain loan products, instead of what would be best for me.

“I’m not a complainer – instead, I saw opportunity. I was sure that a mortgage company that was truly committed to superior customer service and an efficient loan origination process would succeed. When I met Jannie and found she had the same commitment, EON was born.”

When Steve has time off, he enjoys sports and outdoor activities, meeting new people and hearing their stories.

Joseph Agnello

Attorney At Law

After serving in the United States Air Force, Joe graduated from the University of Illinois at Chicago with a degree in history. Following that, he went on to The John Marshall Law School, where he graduated in 2002, and entered the Illinois bar later that year.

Joe’s career started as a prosecutor for the Cook County State's Attorney's Office, beginning with criminal appeals and child support enforcement and then criminal prosecutions. In 2005, Joe changed directions and entered private practice, where he has concentrated his practice in real estate transactions, but has also successfully defended hundreds of criminal and

traffic cases. Joe greatly enjoy the challenge of a court room.

I am a Christian first and foremost, before attorney or any other title, and a family man second. The legal advice I give and the steps I take in representing my clients, are all based on the principles that those two titles embrace. My view in all types of representation that I undertake, whether assisting in the selling or buying of a home or business, or defending someone on significant criminal charges, is to address my clients' issues and concerns as I would want to be represented. In other words, I see my clients as real people, who have come to me for help, not just because they require a lawyer's involvement. Above all, I seek to honor God through my practice, and a portion of the legal fees my office receives goes to support various Christian ministries, primarily in the Chicagoland area.
